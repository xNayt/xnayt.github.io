Helios TTT Rules

 

These rules attempt to cover the most basic rules that you'll see in TTT servers. Its still important to read them so you know how the server operates

Please use common sense, if a rule isn't explicitly stated it can still be implicitly enforced.

The word of the highest ranking staff member online is law

 

General rules

 

1. Be respectful and civil with eachother

2. Do not deathmatch, this is not Call of Duty

3. Do not ghost/metagame

4. Do not kill AFKs, they will be automatically slain

5. Do not traitor bait (eg: shooting randomly or saying things so people will RDM you)

6. Do not prop kill but you CAN prop block and prop surf

7. No offensive or inappropriate sprays

8. Do not falsely report players

9. RDM against a Traitor is still RDM

10. You can kill on reasonable suspicion during overtime

11. No inappropriate sprays. This is entirely up to staff discretion

 

Role rules

 

Non-Detective roles may only claim during overtime. You MUST type this in chat otherwise it isn't valid

Non-Detectives cannot force Innocents to test under the threat of death

Non-Detectives cannot call a "Live check or KOS"

Traitors who die from a fellow traitors C4, its your fault.

Traitors must warn their fellow Traitors (and allow them to move) if they plan to jihad in an area

Detectives may not grapple abuse (constantly grapple or grapple to unreachable areas)

Detectives cannot order other terrorists to harm themselves

Detectives cannot use stupid methods to test players like spelling Innocent backwards

 

Name rules

 

Do not have any names that will disrupt gameplay like "Nobody", "Innocent", or "The World"

Your name should not be too similar to other players in the server 

Your name must be easily pronouncable and use Latin (ones available on a qwerty keyboard) characters

Do not attempt impersonate other players

 

Text rules

 

Do not advertise your community or product

No malicious or screamer links

Do not spam chat with binds

Do not tell people to kill themselves

Do not be homophobic, racist, or otherwise offensive to large groups of people

 

Voice rules

 

All the text rules apply

Do not play music over the microphone

No voice changers

No soundboards

No screaming, yelling, or singing. Anything loud and disruptive isn't allowed

 

Map rules

 

Turning off the lights is KOS unless the Detective says otherwise. The Detective CANNOT force the lights off

Glitching the Richland laser trap is bannable

Hindering the ability to achieve an objective by throwing away key items or breaking testers is KOS

Using the axe on Rooftops to kill people is prop kill

Don't spam the explosive barrel spawner

Don't exploit the map in any manner. Yes this is a blanket statement to stop idiots
